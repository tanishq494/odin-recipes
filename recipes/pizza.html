Classic Pizza Recipe

This classic pizza recipe is simple, delicious, and loved by people worldwide.

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-made pizza base
  • 1/2 cup of pizza sauce
  • 1 cup of mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, olives, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (430°F).
  2. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the base.
  3. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  4. Add desired toppings and bake for 12-15 minutes.
