Gajar Ka Halwa Recipe

Gajar Ka Halwa, a beloved Indian dessert, is made with grated carrots, milk, and sugar. It's rich, delicious, and perfect for special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of grated carrots
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of ghee
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and saut√© the grated carrots for 5 minutes.
  2. Add milk and cook on medium heat until the milk is absorbed.
  3. Stir in sugar and continue cooking until the mixture thickens.
  4. Add cardamom powder and mix well.
  5. Garnish with nuts and serve warm.
